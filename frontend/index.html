About NutriSnap

NutriSnap is a smart food assistant that helps you understand the nutritional content and health implications of your food choices. Our AI-powered analysis provides:

  • Food Group Classification: Identify which food group your meal belongs to
  • Health Tags: Get instant tags like "high protein", "low fat", "junk food", or "healthy"
  • Nutritional Analysis: Comprehensive breakdown of calories, macronutrients, and micronutrients
  • AI Insights: Personalized recommendations and health insights

Make informed decisions about your diet and improve your eating habits with science-backed analysis.
